About UNIBITES

Smart University Canteen Management System

What is UNIBITES?

UNIBITES is a smart, web-based canteen management and food ordering system developed specifically for university and college campuses. It aims to digitalize traditional canteen operations by allowing students to browse food items, place orders online, and reduce waiting time.

Why UNIBITES?

Traditional canteen systems often involve long queues, manual order handling, and delays in service. UNIBITES solves these problems by automating the ordering process and providing real-time food availability and order tracking.

Key Features

📱 Online Ordering

Students can browse food items and place orders anytime from the system.

⚙️ Admin Control

Admins can manage food items, categories, prices, and availability.

🚀 Fast Service

Reduces waiting time and improves efficiency in canteen operations.

🔐 Secure System

Role-based login for admin, students, and staff members.

Conclusion

UNIBITES provides a complete digital solution for university canteens by enhancing efficiency, accuracy, and user experience. It modernizes food ordering and management, making campus dining smarter and more convenient.
