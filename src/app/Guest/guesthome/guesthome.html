authentic kerala food

Experience the rich culinary heritage of Kerala. From the aromatic spices of Malabar cuisine to the comforting traditional breakfasts loved across the state, UNIBITES brings you freshly prepared dishes crafted with authentic ingredients, bold flavors, and the warmth of Kerala hospitality.

Kerala Porotta
Popular

Flaky, layered flatbread made with refined flour and grilled to perfection. Best enjoyed with spicy beef curry or rich chicken gravy.

Appam & Stew
Classic

Soft lacy rice pancakes paired with creamy coconut milk stew. A traditional Kerala breakfast loved for its delicate texture and mild flavors.

Malabar Biryani
Special

Fragrant basmati rice layered with spiced chicken, caramelized onions, and roasted cashews. A true Malabar delicacy packed with rich aroma.

Idiyappam
Healthy

Steamed rice noodles served soft and light. Perfect with egg curry, chicken curry, or sweet coconut milk.

Kerala Sadya
Traditional

A grand vegetarian feast served on a banana leaf with rice, sambar, avial, thoran, pickles, and payasam — the pride of Kerala festivals.

Pazham Pori
Snack

Ripe banana fritters dipped in golden batter and deep fried until crispy outside and soft inside. The perfect Kerala tea-time snack.
